About Our Location

Our bakery is located in the heart of Phnom Penh. We’re a cozy, family-run shop that welcomes you with the smell of fresh bread and the warmth of a friendly smile.

What to Expect

When you step into our bakery, you’ll find a welcoming atmosphere, rustic decor, and shelves filled with freshly baked breads, cakes, and pastries. You can relax with a coffee in our small seating area or take your goodies to go!
